The Center For Business Analytics

  • UNH Center for Business Analytics, in partnership with Peter Zaimes, is launching a Sports Analytics Lab in Fall 2023.
  • The lab will engage up to 15-20 students in practical applications of sports data for operational decisions, talent recruitment, and competitive strategies.
  • The program welcomes UNH students for credit and experience, faculty involvement, and partnerships with athletic and business organizations.
  • With nearly 30 years in quantitative analysis and strategy, Peter will lead the lab and offer deep insights into business statistics and analytics to Paul College students.

Our Goals

  • Offer courses that integrate business analytics with sports management, emphasizing the application of data science in sports.
  • Lead a team of students through engagement models where they can apply data analytics to real-world sports operations and talent recruitment.
  • Provide Paul College students with business insights from various perspectives, preparing them for successful careers in sports analytics.
  • Encourage collaboration among faculty, students, and industry professionals to foster innovation and critical thinking within the Sports Analytics Lab.

For Students

  • Equip students with hands-on experience in sports analytics, preparing them for real-world challenges in data-driven decision-making within the sports industry.
  • Provide opportunities for students to earn academic credit while engaging deeply with the practical and theoretical aspects of sports analytics.
  • Offer a corporate consulting project capstone for undergraduate and MBA students, allowing them to work on actual business problems and develop strategic solutions.
  • Benefit from Peter Zaimes' near three-decade expertise in quantitative analysis, gaining unique insights and mentorship in sports data analysis and application.

For Clients

  • Forge strategic alliances with businesses and athletic organizations to enhance the practical learning experience for students and provide value to partners through data insights and analytics.
  • Engage with clients through consulting projects where students can offer fresh perspectives and data-driven solutions to real-world sports industry challenges.
  • Help clients make informed decisions by analyzing performance metrics, business operations, and recruitment data to gain a competitive edge.
  • Create a collaborative space where clients and students can share ideas, innovate, and tackle complex problems using the power of sports analytics.
